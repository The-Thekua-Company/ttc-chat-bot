# Refund and Returns Policy — The Thekua Company

Source: https://thekuacompany.com/refund_returns/

---

## Overview

Thekua is a **perishable food product** made fresh after each order. Because of this, the returns policy is specific to food items and is different from non-food e-commerce.

---

## Eligibility for Refund or Replacement

Refunds or replacements are considered in the following cases:

1. **Damaged product on delivery** — If the product arrives physically damaged (broken pieces, packaging damage that has affected the food)
2. **Wrong product delivered** — If you received a different size or variant than what was ordered
3. **Quality issue** — If the product is stale, has an off smell, or is clearly unfit for consumption upon arrival

### What is NOT eligible for return:
- Change of mind after delivery
- Dislike of taste (subjective preference)
- Products that have been partially consumed
- Claims made after 48 hours of delivery without photo/video evidence

---

## How to Raise a Refund or Replacement Request

1. Contact the team **within 48 hours of delivery**
2. Send **photo or video proof** of the issue clearly showing the damaged/defective product
3. Include your **order number**
4. Contact via email: **support@thekuacompany.com** or WhatsApp: **+91 8904993884**

The team will review your request and respond. If approved, a replacement or refund will be processed.

---

## Refund Processing

- Approved refunds are returned to the **original payment method**
- Processing time: **5–7 business days** after approval (bank processing times may vary)
- If you don't see the refund after 7 business days, check your bank account, then contact your bank or card issuer before reaching out to us

---

## Order Cancellations

- **Before preparation begins:** You may cancel your order and receive a full refund. Contact us immediately at support@thekuacompany.com or +91 8904993884.
- **After preparation has begun or after dispatch:** Cancellation is not possible as the product has been freshly made to your order.

Since every pack is made fresh after the order, preparation typically begins within 24 hours of order placement.

---

## Exchanges

Exchanges are considered only if:
- The wrong item was delivered
- The delivered item is defective or damaged

To request an exchange, follow the same process as a refund request (within 48 hours, with photo/video evidence).
