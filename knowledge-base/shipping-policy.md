# Shipping Policy — The Thekua Company

Source: https://thekuacompany.com/shipping-policy/

---

## How Shipping Works

Every pack of Thekua is made **fresh after your order is placed**. This means there is a preparation window before dispatch — orders are not dispatched from pre-made stock.

**Preparation time:** 1–3 business days (the time taken by homemakers to prepare the batch after the order is received)  
**Dispatch:** After preparation is complete  
**Delivery after dispatch:** 5–12 days, depending on location across India

### Total estimated time from order to delivery:
- **Minimum:** ~6–8 days (faster delivery zones)
- **Maximum:** ~15 days (remote locations)

---

## Delivery Coverage

Thekua is delivered **pan-India** — across all states and union territories in India.

---

## Packaging

Each jar is packed **airtight** to retain freshness and crunch during transit. The packaging is designed to protect the Thekua from moisture, humidity, and breakage during courier handling.

---

## Shipping Costs

Shipping is calculated at checkout based on delivery location and order size.

---

## Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, tracking details will be shared. You can use the tracking number to follow your order's journey.

---

## Delays

Because every batch is made fresh after the order, slight delays may occur during:
- High-demand periods (Chhath Puja, Diwali, festive seasons)
- Remote or difficult-to-reach delivery locations

If there is a significant delay, the team will proactively communicate.

## Important Notes

- Thekua is a food item prepared fresh — it cannot be "cancelled after dispatch" in the same way non-perishable products can. Please refer to the Refund & Returns Policy for order cancellation windows.
- The 60–75 day shelf life begins from when the product is made, not from when it is ordered. Because it is made after the order, the full shelf life is ahead of you when it arrives.
